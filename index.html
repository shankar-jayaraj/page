Profile


  He is working as Assistant Professor in the Electronics and Communication Engineering Department at UKF College of Engineering and Technology, Kollam, which is affiliated to A P J Abdul Kalam Technological University (formerly Kerala Technological University) with a total of 8 years of teaching and research experience and two years experience in the telecom industry.


  He is an executive committee member of IEEE Kerala Section presently serving as SAC and has exercised various section volunteering roles. He has also served as a steering committee member of various IEEE international and national conferences/events hosted by the IEEE Asia Pacific Region (R-10) and IEEE Kerala Section.


  He is an active IEEE Volunteer, also a Mentor of Change of Atal Tinkering Lab, AIM, NITI Aayog, Govt of India, and a licensed Amateur Radio Operator.


  He is also a Chief Technical Officer (CTO), Quolabs.io, Thiruvananthapuram.

Education


   He completed his B.Tech in Electronics and Communication from the University College of Engineering (UCE), Kariavattom (University of Kerala), in 2009. After two years of industry experience in the telecom sector. He completed his M.Tech in Communication Systems from SRM University, Chennai, in the year 2013.


   His paper “Combined Utility and Adaptive Residence Time Based Network Selection algorithm for 4G Wireless Networks” received the Springer best paper award during the 3rd International Conference on Multimedia Technology (ICMT 2013), Guangzhou, China. In addition, it was published in Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering as a Springer Book Chapter.


   His area of interest is wireless communication, Robotics, the Internet of Things, Software Defined Radio, Amateur Radio. His research interest is in Brain-Computer Interface.
